Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta

Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta is a quick and flavorful dish that combines tender shrimp with perfectly cooked pasta, all tossed in rich, garlicky butter sauce. It's a comforting and elegant meal that's easy enough for weekniight dinner but impressive enough for guests. The blend of garlic, butter, and shrimp creates a savory, mouthwatering taste that's hard to resist.

Ingredients

  • 200g (7oz) spagehetti
  • 250g (9oz) shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Optional: salt, pepper, parsley, chili flakes, lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Boil Spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan over medium heat, melt 1 tablespoon of butter. Add shrimp, season with salt and pepper, and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until pink adn cookedd through. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt the remaining 2 tablesppons of butter. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. (Add chili flakes if desired.)
  4. Add the cooked pasta and shrimp back into the pan. Toss to coat everything in the garlic butter sauce. Garnish with chopped parselt and a squeeze of lemon juice if you like. Serve warm.
